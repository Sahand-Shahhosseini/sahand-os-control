## Reviewer loop
1) Prepare RC0 (artifact exists; not placeholder)
2) Send to an external reviewer
3) Record feedback (text/screenshot/email)
4) Log feedback into DOC14 (Reviewer Feedback Log)
5) Convert feedback into Issues
6) Apply fixes and publish RC1
7) Repeat until APPROVED_EXT
8) Freeze and tag LOCKED_v0.1

## Evidence requirement
- No APPROVED_EXT without real feedback evidence.
- No LOCKED_v0.1 without APPROVED_EXT + applied fixes.
